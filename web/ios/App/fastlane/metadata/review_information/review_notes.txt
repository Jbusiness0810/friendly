Friendly is a social app for meeting neighbors and making platonic friends.

To test the app:
1. Sign up with email or use Sign in with Apple/Google
2. Complete onboarding (name, interests, location)
3. Browse the home feed to see nearby users
4. Tap "Wave" to send a connection request
5. If the other person waves back, you can chat
6. Check the Events tab to see/create local events
7. Visit Profile to edit your info or delete your account
