Friendly makes it easy to meet real people in your neighborhood. No swiping, no algorithms — just genuine connections with the people around you.

HOW IT WORKS

Wave at someone nearby to break the ice. If they wave back, you're matched and can start chatting. Find common interests, make plans, and turn neighbors into friends.

FEATURES

• Wave to Connect — Send a friendly wave to people nearby. When they wave back, you're matched.
• Real Profiles — Share your interests, ideal hangouts, and what you're looking for. No fake personas.
• Local Events — Discover and create neighborhood events like pickup basketball, coffee meetups, and BBQs.
• Circles — Join local interest groups to find your people.
• Chat — Message your matches to plan hangouts and build friendships.
• Verified Profiles — Optional identity verification so you know who you're meeting.

WHY FRIENDLY?

Making friends as an adult is hard. Dating apps don't cut it when you just want someone to grab a beer with or hit the gym. Friendly is built for platonic connection — meeting the people who live around you and actually hanging out.

Whether you just moved to a new city, want a gym partner, or are looking for people to watch the game with, Friendly helps you find your crew.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your data stays yours. We don't sell your information or show you ads. Location is only used to connect you with nearby people and is never shared publicly.
